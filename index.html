Bread omelette

easy way of making bread omelette for meal this contains two breads and two omlelette

Preparation time

  • Total time : 10 mins
  • preparation : 5mins
  • Cooking : 5mins

Ingredients

  • 2-3 Breads
  • 2-3 Omlettes
  • 2 table spoons of oil or butter
  • salt for taste
  • masalas for taste
  • optional veggies like onions,green chilles

INSTRUCTIONS

    INSTRUCTIONS
  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add anything else you would like in the omelet.
  3. Add the splash of milk and whisk until yolks are broken.
  4. Put the bowl in the microwave for 1 minute.(or longer depending on your watts).
  5. Check on the bowl every 30 seconds.
  6. Peel the string cheese.
  7. Take out the bowl and put the omelet on a plate.
  8. Sprinkle on the cheese and put in the microwave for 30 seconds so cheese can melt.
  9. Take out plate and drizzle on ketchup, hot sauce, or salsa.
  10. ENJOY!
